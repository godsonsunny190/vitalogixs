Our Motivation For Starting Vitalogixs, Inc.

Over the years, my wife and I have witnessed intimately the profound impact that serious health issues can have on individuals and families. Our personal experiences have ranged from battling conditions such as Crohn's disease and cancer to facing challenges related to both physical illness and mental health.

With almost three decades of combined experience in health, wellness, and patient care, we became acutely aware of the significant gap that exists between what individuals truly need and what the healthcare industry provides. This gap became even more evident during my own battle with cancer. Throughout this journey, I closely examined not only how patients like myself were treated, but also how our family members' diverse health issues were managed.

We identified several critical shortcomings in the current healthcare landscape: inadequate patient care and follow-up, unreliable information regarding progressive alternative treatments, substantial product quality issues, and poor customer service. These deficiencies motivated us to take action.

2018
Founding Year

Vitalogixs, Inc. was founded by healthcare professionals and patient advocates who recognized the critical gaps in modern healthcare. Our mission began with a commitment to bridge the divide between patient needs and industry offerings through compassionate, comprehensive care.

2020
Telehealth Expansion

Amidst global health challenges, we launched our nationwide telehealth services, providing accessible healthcare to patients across the country. This expansion allowed us to connect individuals with specialized healthcare professionals regardless of geographic limitations.

2022
Integrated Services Launch

We expanded our offerings to include comprehensive laboratory and pharmacy solutions, creating a seamless healthcare ecosystem. Our integrated approach ensures patients receive coordinated care from diagnosis through treatment with the highest quality pharmaceutical and nutraceutical products.
